Benefits




The Healthcare industry remains among the fastest to adopt the Internet of Things.
The reason for this trend is that integrating IoT features into medical devices greatly improves the quality and effectiveness of service, bringing especially high value for the elderly, patients with chronic conditions, and those requiring constant supervision.
According to some estimates, spending on the Healthcare IoT solutions will reach a staggering $1 trillion by 2025 and, hopefully, will set the stage for highly personalized, accessible, and on-time Healthcare services for everyone.

How

User interface

There will be a user interface for all the authorized medical staff in hospitals. It will be easy to use in a way that anybody that is not familiar with computers should be able to use it. Everybody will get a connection ID and password randomly generated by the system. This user interface is the platform that allows staff to access to patients medical records


Accessing records

There will be two ways to interact with patients medical records: reading over and adding/changing content. Thanks to the UI, the system will grant you the possibility either to only read over or read over and add/change content. The system will be able to identify the patient and give to his doctor his medical records

Limited and secure access

Depending on the medical statue, there will be different rights, action possibilities over the system


Unexplored possibilities

One possibility of a health intelligence intelligence center to a completely different level. Using artificial intelligence, tools that no longer allow errors
